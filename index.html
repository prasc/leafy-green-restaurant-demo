Founded in 2010, Leafy Greens is a destination for innovative plant-based cuisine in Toronto.

Leafy Green's vision goes beyond taking animals off the plate — it exists to bring guests an uncompromising restaurant experience that champions environmental sustainability.

New Takes On Old Classics – feel good while doing good, all while eating 100% vegan.

It’s straight-up favourites with a familiar twist. No preservatives. Never frozen or artificial. Just fresh from the farm vegetables and high-quality ingredients transformed into meals that are going to make you feel good and leave you satifised. Eating should always be this fun.
